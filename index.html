How to help Ukraine

The Russian invasion into Ukraine has shocked us as much as you. Here's what we can do.

Ways to Help: The most important things

Note: this site is still under construction as you can see. While we get up and running, check out this list already and feel free to check the github linked in the footer to help us improve!

The main ways that you can help are by contacting the politician(s) that represent you, donating to charities that aim to help Ukraine, and educating yourself and others to help combat the spread of Russian propaganda and misinformation.
